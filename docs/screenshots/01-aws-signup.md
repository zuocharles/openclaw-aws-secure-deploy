## Overview

You need an AWS account to host your OpenClaw server. This guide walks you through creating one.

**Estimated time:** 5-10 minutes  
**Cost:** AWS Free Tier covers this for 12 months (750 hours/month of t2.micro/t3.micro)

---

## Step 1.1: Go to AWS Signup

1. Visit: https://aws.amazon.com/free/
2. Click "Create a Free Account"

**[SCREENSHOT NEEDED]:** AWS homepage with "Create a Free Account" button highlighted

## Step 1.2: Enter Email and Account Name

1. Enter your email address
2. Choose an account name (e.g., "openclaw-server")
3. Click "Verify email address"

**[SCREENSHOT NEEDED]:** Email verification page

## Step 1.3: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email for the verification code
2. Enter the code
3. Click "Verify"

## Troubleshooting

### "My credit card was declined"
- Make sure the billing address matches your card
- Try a different card
- Contact your bank (they may block international transactions)

### "I didn't receive the verification SMS"
- Check your spam folder
- Try the "Call me" option instead
- Make sure your phone can receive international SMS

### "Account verification is taking too long"
- Sometimes AWS manually reviews new accounts
- Can take up to 24 hours
- You'll receive an email when approved
